About

Inspired by a father’s fond memories of reading to his daughter, the HMR team rallied together to create a platform where friends, family, loved ones, and classmates alike can read and listen to one another. The mission of HMR is to enrich people’s lives with the joys of storytelling. We hope this app brings every subscriber a level of joy that can be shared with everyone in their network!
